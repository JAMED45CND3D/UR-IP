SPIRAL MODEL

PARAMS a=0.15, b=0.18, θ: 0→8π
OFFSET S1=0° · S2=120° · S3=240°
NODES LITE:60 · STD:150 · DENSE:360 per spiral
ENCODE node besar (r=4.5) = 1 · kecil (r=1.8) = 0
